CLASSIC LEMONADE
Ingredients:
- 6 lemons, juiced (about 1 cup of juice)
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 6 cups cold water
- Ice cubes
- Lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:
1. In a small saucepan, combine sugar with 1 cup of water. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves completely.
2. Remove from heat and let the simple syrup cool to room temperature.
3. In a large pitcher, combine lemon juice, simple syrup, and remaining 5 cups of cold water.
4. Stir well and refrigerate until chilled.
5. Serve over ice with lemon slices as garnish.

GRILLED CORN ON THE COB
Ingredients:
- 6 ears of fresh corn, husks on
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (such as chives, parsley, or basil), chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Soak corn in cold water for 30 minutes.
2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
3. In a small bowl, mix butter with herbs, garlic, salt, and pepper.
4. Pull back husks but leave them attached, remove silk from corn.
5. Spread herb butter over each ear of corn, then pull husks back over corn.
6. Grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.
7. Serve with additional herb butter if desired.

SUMMER BERRY SMOOTHIE
Ingredients:
- 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
- 1 banana
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:
1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

WATERMELON FETA SALAD
Ingredients:
- 6 cups watermelon, cubed
- 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine watermelon, feta, mint, and red onion.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
3. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss gently.
4. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.
